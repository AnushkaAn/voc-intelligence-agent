## Global Action Item Report

### For Product Team

#### MasterBuds Issues to Fix

* **Noise Cancellation Not Working Properly**: Implement a more robust noise cancellation algorithm to address customer complaints of poor noise cancellation, both indoors and outdoors.
* **Sound Quality Not Competing with JBL and Sony**: Improve sound quality to match or exceed that of competitors, particularly in terms of bass and mid-range frequencies.
* **Battery Life and Charging Issues**: Address customer complaints of short battery life and charging issues, such as the inability to wear the earbuds for more than 30 minutes without needing to recharge.

#### MasterBudsMax Issues to Fix

* **Mids Overpowering Bass in Some Songs**: Refine the audio tuning to prevent mids from overpowering bass in certain tracks, ensuring a more balanced sound experience.
* **Earbud Cushion Durability**: Improve the durability of the earbud cushions to prevent them from being damaged by ants or other external factors.
